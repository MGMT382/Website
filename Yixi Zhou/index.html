Passport and Visa

International travel requires a visa to go through Customs. Please check whether your passport has expired before purchasing our travel products. If your passport is closed to expired or already expired, please renew it as soon as possible. For details, please visit Travel.State.Gov for more relevant information. We also provide services to help renew passports and happy to help you.

If your passport is within the validity date, then we will provide you with tourist visa services to ensure that you can safely enter the your destination country. Depending on the country, visa requirements will vary. It is recommended to apply for the service two or three months before departure.

If you have any of the above service needs or questions, please contact me.





Money Exchange

The currency of different countries is different, so remember to exchange some money of your destination country before departure. Depending on the exchange rate fluctuations, the price will vary slightly. Please contact the bank in advance to exchange the required currency, or you can apply for a Global Debit Card. If you have any questions or needs, please feel free to contact me.
